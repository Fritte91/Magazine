The Journey Under the Waves

January 15, 2024 By Beam Klongluang

The journey of creating this magazine began like diving into unknown waters. Each interview, each story uncovered, brought new depths of understanding about Thailand's cannabis culture.

The Beginning

When we first conceived the idea for Now Or Never Magazine, we knew we wanted to create something different. Something that would capture not just the emerging cannabis culture in Thailand, but the deep spiritual and healing traditions that have been part of our society for generations.

Meeting the Community

Our journey took us to remote villages where traditional healers have been working with cannabis for generations. We met farmers who have adapted ancient growing techniques for the modern era, and spiritual leaders who shared insights about the plant's role in meditation and healing practices.

"Cannabis isn't just a plant in Thai culture - it's a bridge between our ancient wisdom and modern understanding."

The Creative Process

Each page of the magazine was carefully crafted to reflect the authenticity of the stories we discovered. We wanted readers to feel the same sense of wonder and discovery that we experienced during our journey.
