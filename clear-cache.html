Clear Service Worker Cache

Use this tool to clear the service worker cache if you're experiencing caching issues with your website.

Manual Instructions:

  1. Open Chrome DevTools (F12)
  2. Go to Application tab
  3. Click "Service Workers" in left sidebar
  4. Click "Unregister" for your site
  5. Go to "Storage" in left sidebar
  6. Click "Clear storage" button
  7. Refresh the page (Ctrl+F5)
