Bob Marley

A global icon who helped spread the message of cannabis spirituality and consciousness. His influence on cannabis culture and reggae music created a lasting legacy that continues to inspire millions around the world.

Ko Dam Koh Tao

A key figure in Thailand's cannabis scene, known for popularizing the KD Koh Tao strain, a local strain from Koh Tao Island. He is also widely known and recognized for supporting and educating local communities, and the development of Thailand's cannabis culture.

Dr.Highthai

A voice of sharing within Thailand's cannabis culture, P'K has spent over 20 years growing, teaching, and sharing his craft with an open heart. With passion and a free spirit, he continues to grow and cultivate the community, rooted in love and knowledge.

Kitty Chopaka

A prominent cannabis advocate in Thailand who played a crucial role in the legalization movement. Her work continues to shape the emerging legal cannabis industry in Southeast Asia.

The Author

Phonotype_247

Cannabis Culture Journalist & Advocate

My Journey

Hello, I'm Phonotype_247, the author of Now or Never magazine. For a long time, I've always been in love with cannabis and the cultures surrounding it. Ever since I got to know and use it, many things in my life changed. It has helped me connect more deeply with myself and others, and expand myself to be more in tune with the surroundings.

Creative Impact

In artistic fields like music and visual arts, it has enhanced both my enjoyment and the creative process, allowing me to connect and learn from others more. The plant has opened new doors of perception and creativity that I never knew existed.

The Mission

With my background in photography, cannabis media design, and personal experience as a grower, I created the Now or Never magazine to share this journey—in hopes that others will be able to learn more about cannabis and come to appreciate it like I have.

Vision

As the founder of Now Or Never Magazine, I bring together my passion for cannabis culture, deep understanding of traditional Thai medicine, and commitment to responsible advocacy to create a publication that bridges the gap between traditional wisdom and modern cannabis culture.
