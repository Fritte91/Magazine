Meeting the Legends

February 3, 2024 By Beam Klongluang

From remote mountain villages to bustling city dispensaries, each legend I met shared their unique perspective on cannabis culture. Their stories weaved together like ancient tapestries, revealing the rich history of Thailand's relationship with this sacred plant.

The Mountain Sage

High in the mountains of northern Thailand, I met with Uncle Som, a third-generation cannabis cultivator. His family's techniques have been passed down through generations, carefully preserved like the rare landrace strains they protect.

The Urban Pioneer

In Bangkok, I spent time with Dr. Somchai, one of the first advocates for medical cannabis in Thailand. His clinic has become a beacon of hope for patients seeking alternative treatments.

"We're not just preserving plants; we're preserving wisdom that could help future generations."

The Future Guardians

The most inspiring part of this journey was meeting young entrepreneurs who are bridging traditional knowledge with modern science, ensuring that Thailand's cannabis heritage evolves while staying true to its roots.
